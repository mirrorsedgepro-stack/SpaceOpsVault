# Key Russian EW Systems

> [!abstract] Quick Summary
> Details Russia's key space-targeting EW systems — Krasukha, Tirada, Tobol, and Zhitel — their technical characteristics and how they were employed in the Ukraine conflict. Operationally relevant because these systems directly threaten GPS, SATCOM, and ISR links that ADF and coalition forces depend on.

| System | Type | Range | Bands | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Krasukha-2** (1L269) | Ground EW | ~250 km | S-band | Targets AWACS radar |
| **Krasukha-4** (1RL257) | Ground EW | ~300 km | X/Ku/Ka | SATCOM and radar; deployed Syria/Ukraine; **supplied to Iran Aug 2025** |
| **Tirada-2S** | SATCOM uplink jammer | — | SATCOM uplink | Targets [[SATCOM Architecture\|WGS]] and allied SATCOM |
| **Tobol** | GNSS jammer | 100+ km | GPS/GNSS | Creates persistent denial zones |
| **R-330Zh Zhitel** | Tactical mobile jammer | — | SATCOM/GNSS | Brigade-level asset |
| **Pole-21** | Cell-tower mounted GNSS jammer | — | GPS | Creates GPS denial perimeter around fixed sites |
| **Murmansk-BN** | HF jammer | 5,000 km | HF | Can jam [[Link 22]] HF sky-wave |

> [!tip] Hot Tip
> Krasukha-4 can reportedly suppress drone reconnaissance at 150-300km — in any scenario involving Russian EW, assume GPS-dependent ISR platforms within that radius are degraded. This directly affects ADF ISR platforms operating in coalition with partners facing Russian EW.

## Operational Context

- Russia has integrated EW at **brigade level** as daily force protection in Ukraine
- GPS jamming/spoofing expanded dramatically since 2024 across Baltic, Nordic, Black Sea, Middle East
- See [[EW Against Data Links]] for how these systems target [[Link 16]], [[CEC and NIFC-CA|CEC]], and [[SATCOM Architecture|SATCOM]]

## Iran Technology Transfer

- Krasukha-4 supplied to Iran August 2025
