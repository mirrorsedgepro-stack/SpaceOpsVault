# US Counterspace (Offensive)

> [!abstract] Quick Summary
> Covers acknowledged US offensive counterspace capabilities — the Counter Communications System (CCS), Meadowlands, and the space jamming triad — representing the other side of the counterspace equation. Understanding these systems helps ADF personnel contextualise US rules of engagement and the deliberate preference for reversible, non-kinetic effects.

## CCS (Counter Communications System)

- **16 mobile SATCOM jammer platforms**
- Operational since 2004
- Reversible effect (jamming, not kinetic)
- Sustained [[Orbital Mechanics|GEO]] denial capability

## Meadowlands (CCS Block 10.2+)

- Next-gen replacement for CCS
- Contractor: L3Harris
- **First two units delivered April 2025**
- Smaller, more mobile, higher jam-to-signal performance
- Targets Russian (Rodnik-S, Meridian-M) and Chinese ([[BeiDou B3A and SMC|BeiDou]], military SATCOM) constellations

> [!tip] Hot Tip
> CCS and Meadowlands are reversible, non-kinetic systems — the US deliberately developed these to provide a counterspace option that doesn't create debris or permanent escalation. When advising commanders on counterspace options, this is why the threshold for kinetic ASAT is deliberately very high.

## USSF Jamming Triad

| Element | Role |
| --- | --- |
| **CCS** | Sustained GEO denial |
| **Meadowlands** | Mobile/agile ISR denial |
| **RMT** | Dispersed/remotely cued |

> The triad provides layered, complementary offensive EW capability against adversary space systems while maintaining reversibility (no debris).

---

> [!warning]- Constraints, Limitations and Assumptions
> **Constraints:** US offensive counterspace operations are governed by strict legal review, NCA authorisation requirements, and information operations considerations — these are not tools that field commanders can request and receive quickly.
>
> **Limitations:** Jamming is effective only within geometry and power constraints — a high-power, directional jammer still requires line-of-sight and frequency knowledge.
>
> **Assumptions:** Assumes adversaries have not yet developed effective countermeasures to CCS/Meadowlands-type jamming — electronic warfare is an ongoing race.
