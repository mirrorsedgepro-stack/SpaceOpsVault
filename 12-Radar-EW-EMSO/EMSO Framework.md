# EMSO (Electromagnetic Spectrum Operations) Framework

> [!abstract] Quick Summary
> Describes the EMSO framework — JP 3-85, Australia's DEMSS, and lessons from Ukraine — situating spectrum as a warfighting domain requiring deliberate planning. ADF staff must understand both US and Australian EMSO structures to operate effectively in joint and coalition environments.

## US Framework

- **JP 3-85** (Joint EMSO)
- **JEMSOC** (Joint EMS Operations Cell): coordinates EW/spectrum/SIGINT/cyber/space EW

## ADF Framework

- **DEMSS** (Defence Electromagnetic Spectrum Strategy)
- Joint EMSO Cell at HQJOC
- Defence Spectrum Office (Russell ACT)
- **7th Signal Regiment EW** (Cabarlah QLD)

## Key ADF Programmes

| Programme | Status |
| --- | --- |
| JP 9321 Electromagnetic Battle Management (**Jemstone**) | Operational **Dec 2025** |
| **mySPECTRA** | Operational **March 2026** |
| Sea 5011 Maritime EMW | In development |

## ADF Capability Gap

> EW budget ~$720M = **0.78% of $92B IIP**
> Tactical comms not fully hardened; insufficient EMS specialists.

## Ukraine Lessons Learned

- Russia integrated EW at **brigade level** as daily force protection
- Ukraine neutralised 4,652 drones May–Jul 2025 through EW + kinetic combined
- **Key takeaway**: EW must integrate to **company/platoon level**

> [!tip] Hot Tip
> Ukraine demonstrated that EMSO is a two-sided contest that evolves in days, not months — Russian and Ukrainian EW tactics changed faster than doctrine could capture. When applying EMSO lessons, distinguish between what worked in Ukraine's specific geography and threat environment and what translates to Indo-Pacific scenarios.

---

> [!warning]- Constraints, Limitations and Assumptions
> **Constraints:** ADF's Defence Electromagnetic Spectrum Strategy (DEMSS) is a strategic document — operational EMSO doctrine for specific platforms and scenarios requires additional JTAC/JEMSO coordination.
>
> **Limitations:** Spectrum management across a joint and coalition force is complex — frequency deconfliction between ADF and USSF systems requires dedicated spectrum managers at each echelon.
>
> **Assumptions:** Assumes adversaries have not pre-surveyed and catalogued Australian/coalition spectrum usage — in a long-duration conflict, assume the adversary knows your emission patterns.
