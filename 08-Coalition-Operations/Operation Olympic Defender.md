# Operation Olympic Defender (OOD)

> [!abstract] Quick Summary
> Details Operation Olympic Defender — the multinational framework for combined space operations led by the US — covering its membership, structure, and what it means for ADF personnel deploying to CSpOC. This is the operational umbrella under which ADF space operators embed at Vandenberg SFB.

## Mission

Optimise space operations, improve mission assurance, enhance resilience, synchronise deterrence, reduce orbital debris.

## 2026 Focus

**Year of Integration** — operationalising a fully integrated joint and allied space warfighting team.

> [!tip] Hot Tip
> OOD is the operational umbrella under which ADF space operators embed at CSpOC. Before any ADF deployment to Vandenberg, understand which OOD nations are currently contributing and at what classification level — this determines who you can share information with at the combined watch floor.

---

> [!warning]- Constraints, Limitations and Assumptions
> **Constraints:** OOD membership and contribution levels are not all publicly disclosed — some partner contributions are classified.
>
> **Limitations:** OOD is a coordination framework, not a command authority — it cannot compel member nations to take action. Each nation retains national caveats.
>
> **Assumptions:** Assumes all OOD member nations share the same adversary threat picture — in practice, national intelligence assessments may differ.
