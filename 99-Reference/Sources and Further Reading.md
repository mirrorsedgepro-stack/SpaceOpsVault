# Sources and Further Reading

> [!abstract] Quick Summary
> The master reference list for primary sources, threat assessments, news outlets, and ADF-specific doctrine — the starting point for any deeper research beyond this vault.

## Primary Sources

- **USSF Doctrine**: Spacepower 2020, SDP 3-0, SDP 3-100 SDA, SDP 3-101 Targeting
- **JP 3-14 Space Operations** (August 2023) — limited distribution (DoD only)
- **JP 3-85 Joint EMSO**
- **Space Warfighting: A Framework for Planners** (April 2025) — codifies USSF shift to contested warfighting domain

## Open-Source Threat Assessments

> [!tip] Hot Tip
> The CSIS Space Threat Assessment and SWF Global Counterspace Capabilities Report are released annually — check for the latest edition before citing either in a brief. Using a year-old threat assessment in a current brief will be noticed by subject matter experts.

- **CSIS Space Threat Assessment 2025** — csis.org/analysis/space-threat-assessment-2025
- **SWF Global Counterspace Capabilities Report 2025** (316 pp) — swfound.org/publications-and-reports/2025-global-counterspace-capabilities-report
- **RAND — Lessons from the War in Ukraine for Space (2025)** — rand.org/pubs/research_reports/RRA2950-1.html
- **Mitchell Institute — Ensuring a Spacepower Advantage in Prolonged Competition (Feb 2025)**
