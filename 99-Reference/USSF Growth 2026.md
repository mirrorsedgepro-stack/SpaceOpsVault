# USSF Growth 2026

> Summarises the key USSF growth and organisational developments of 2026 — including the drive to double the force, the new cyber squadron at Vandenberg, and operational validation from recent strikes.

- New **cyber defence squadron** established at Vandenberg SFB (March 2026) — protecting physical and digital launch infrastructure
